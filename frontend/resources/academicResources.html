Tutoring Resources

ODU Language Learning Center (LLC)

Explore ODU's Language Learning Center, which provides different resources on studying and learning languages as well as an option to book an appointment for assistance with a specific language or culture.

ODU Science Tutoring Center

ODU's Science Tutoring Center offers tutoring in subjects including: Biology, Chemistry, Computer Science, Math & Statistics, Ocean & Earth Science, Physics, and Psychology.

ODU SMART Room

The SMART room tutoring resource at ODU offers tutoring sessions in 101M (Intro to Math for Critical Thinking), 102M (College Algebra), 103M (College Algebra with Supplemental Instruction), and 162M(Precalculus I).

ODU Physics Learning Center

ODU's Physics Learning Center offers a comfortable and convenient in-person study location, in-person tutoring, and online tutoring.

ODU Mathematics & Statistics Tutoring

The Math and Stat tutoring resources offered by ODU includes online zoom help sessions for a variety of courses as well as a directory of Math and Stat faculty members that includes their office hours information.

ODU Writing Resource Center

The Writing Resource Center at ODU provides assistance to undergraduate and graduate students with their writing for any course through online and on-campus appointments.

ODU Collaborative Learning Environment Lab

Learn more about ODU's Hands-On-Learning lab includes tutoring for various courses in Mechanical Engineering on-campus.
